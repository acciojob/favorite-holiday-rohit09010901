Araku Valley

Araku is located in the Eastern Ghats about 114 kilometres from Visakhapatnam. It is known for its rich biodiversity, coffee plantations, and scenic landscapes.


  1. Borra Caves with stunning rock formations
  2. Padmapuram Gardens with beautiful plants
  3. Chaparral Water Cascade with fresh water
  4. Galikonda Viewpoint with a heart-warming view
  5. Coffee Museum with ancient items
  • Bamboo Chicken/Biryani – marinated chicken cooked in bamboo
  • Gutti Vankaya Kura – a classic Andhra dish
  • Pesarattu – green gram dosa, a healthy breakfast item
  • Pulihora – tangy tamarind rice
  • Araku Coffee – famous organic coffee
